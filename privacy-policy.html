Privacy Policy for Jobba

Effective date: 6 July 2026

Jobba is a work time tracking app for Android, developed by Joel Hjalmarsson. This policy describes how Jobba handles your information. The short version: Jobba collects no data and sends nothing anywhere.

Data the app stores

Jobba stores the information you enter while using the app — work sessions (start and stop times), lunch break durations, day notes, and your settings (formats, holiday country, backup schedule). All of this is stored only on your device, in the app's private storage.

Data collection and sharing

Jobba does not collect, transmit, or share any data. Specifically:

  • The app does not request network access and makes no internet connections.
  • No personal data is sent to the developer or to any third party.
  • The app contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.
  • No account or sign-in exists or is required.

Exports and backups

You can export your data (CSV, JSON, or Markdown) and set up automatic backups. These files are written only to locations you choose yourself through Android's system file picker — for example your device storage or your own cloud storage account. The developer never receives or has access to these files. Files you export or back up are under your control, and their privacy is governed by wherever you choose to store them.

Permissions

Jobba requests the following Android permissions:

  • Notifications — to show a timer while a session is running, remind you about unusually long sessions, and report backup results. Optional; the app works without it.
  • Ignore battery optimizations (optional) — only to make scheduled backups run reliably on some devices.

Data retention and deletion

Your data stays on your device until you delete it. You can delete individual sessions in the app, clear the app's data in Android settings, or uninstall the app — any of these removes all locally stored data. Backup files you created in your own storage are unaffected and remain under your control.

Children

Jobba does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.
